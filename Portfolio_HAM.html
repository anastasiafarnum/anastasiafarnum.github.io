Usability And Accessibility In A Museum

a qualitative research study conducted at
the Harvard Art Museums

THE PROCESS


Context:

At the time of this project, there hadn’t been any usability and accessibility run-throughs of the new space/experience created by combining the three Harvard Art Museums. Working under the department of Digital Infrastructures and Emerging Technology, I was tasked with making the first research initiatives.

Opportunity:

How can the museum experience [specifically at the Harvard Art Museums] become more accessible to visitors while aligning with the museum’s educational mission?

Outcome:

I conducted research and testing through various formats focusing on the user’s experience in the following three areas:

+  using mobile devices as a tool within the museum

+  the museum website

+  digital signage and interfaces throughout the galleries in the museum
