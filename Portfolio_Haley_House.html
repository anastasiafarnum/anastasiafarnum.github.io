Communications and Marketing

a sample of work done for the nonprofit Haley House

HALEY HOUSE CAFE

At Haley House I was responsible for creating the majority of the communications materials for the nonprofit during my year long contract. Materials I created were both digital and printed.

Since Haley House is an expansive nonprofit that is very active in the surrounding community, there were lots of events put out by the organization on a regular basis. This meant there was a constant need for flyers and event graphics to get the word out to participants. Some of my favorite flyers to create were for the Haley House Cafe. Pictured are flyers I created for some literary inspired events at the Haley House Cafe.

EVENT BRANDING


Haley House Block Party:

The block party is an annual outdoor event by Haley House that brings the community together over food, games, and activities. The block party also serves as a fundraiser for the nonprofit. Designing for the block party meant creating a whole branding identity that could be used in various places, both digital and printed. After creating the branding identity, I then made flyers, postcards, social media posts, table setting pieces, signage and more all using the same cohesive style.


ANNUAL NEWSLETTER

Once a year, Haley House mails out a physical newsletter full of stories and highlights from the previous year. Each time it goes out, the design and layout gets a fresh look.

I really enjoyed getting to create the design, layout, and custom illustrations for the newsletter. I made digital drawings for the cover and for some of the pages inside such as the leaf shaped timeline and the drawing of the Haley House office. My intention was to create a warm handwritten feeling in the newsletter so that it feels even more like a personal message from the nonprofit itself.
